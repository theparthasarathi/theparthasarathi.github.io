Understanding my guiding principles and methods.

My Philosophy

I hold that our culture is founded on the principle that the individual’s voice is crucial in reshaping tyrannical systems and establishing order. This requires confronting our inner and outer chaos with courage and truth. If we embrace truthful speech, we pave the way for genuine order and progress.

In the stories we live, we encounter structures of society and the individual’s role within them. Success and failure are intertwined with our social environment, and personal growth often requires reducing suffering through self-betterment and responsibility.

You have a sovereign right to your destiny - and the world, since there is no difference between you and the world, they are both the same thing. And the pursuit of impulsive pleasure almost always lead to tyranny; instead, strive for long-term victories through discipline and reciprocity.

My Approach

Action precedes motivation. Begin with what you can control—organize your immediate surroundings and expand outward.

Rule for Business: you don't want your customers to be satisfied, you want your customers to be thrilled. My aim is to create value that resonates deeply, and to lead by the principals of fair play.

Real-time Voice Clonning

📍 Final Year Project- Pragati Mahavidyalaya, India
  • PURPOSE: Demonstrate the feasibility and applications of real-time voice cloning technology.
  • APPROACH: Implemented Corentin Jemine's Real-Time Voice Cloning framework, based on Google's 2018 research. Overcame significant technical challenges, leveraging ChatGPT for troubleshooting. Used three models: embedder, vocoder, and synthesizer.
  • RESULTS: Successfully cloned voices with only five seconds of input, demonstrating the technology's potential to peers and university staff. Highlighted ethical considerations and practical applications, inspiring discussions on the future of voice cloning.
